Gun feel day. ## Phase 1: FPS Viewmodel & ADS Foundation (Apr 18 afternoon)

Built the first-person arms and aiming system from scratch:

- **FPS arms with hands** — Upper arm (sleeved) + forearm + tactical glove fist, parented to GunPivot so they move with the gun during ADS/bob/recoil. Left hand grips front guard, right hand grips pistol grip.
- **Iron sights for all weapons** — Shotgun bead, SMG compact sights, AR flip-up on picatinny rail. DMR/Sniper already had optic scopes.
- **ADS alignment marathon** — Spent ~6 commits getting sights to line up with the HUD crosshair. Key issues: PSX vertex snapping was jittering the viewmodel, iron sight z-fighting with gun body, ADS position drifting right due to exponential lerp never converging to X=0.
- **Hollow scopes** — DMR and Sniper scopes rebuilt as 4 thin BoxMesh panels forming an open tube (can see through when ADS), replacing solid cylinder/box meshes.
- **PSX snap disabled for viewmodel** — Created `_gun_mat()` helper that disables vertex snapping, affine warp, and color quantize for all gun meshes. Solved z-fighting and visual jitter.

## Phase 2: Recoil System (Apr 18 evening)

Rewrote recoil three times to get it right:

- **Problem 1: Recoil was going downward** — `head.rotation.x -= value` goes the opposite direction to `head.rotate_x(value)`. Fixed to use `rotate_x(positive)` for upward kick.
- **Problem 2: Apply and recovery ran same frame** — Kick and recovery cancelled each other out, net recoil was near zero. Added `return` after apply phase so recovery only starts after all pending recoil is applied.
- **Smooth pending-recoil buffer** — Recoil accumulates in a buffer, applied gradually via `recoil_apply_speed`. No more instant camera jerk.
- **Per-weapon multipliers** — SMG 0.5x, AR 1.0x, DMR 1.8x, Shotgun 2.5x, Sniper 4.0x.
- **6 simultaneous feedback systems** — Camera vertical kick, gun model kickback, camera micro-shake, FOV punch, crosshair bloom, gun Z-axis roll.
- **ADS recoil** — Gun kick now actually works in ADS (was being killed by `_update_weapon_bob` every frame). Fixed by letting kick tween play when active instead of force-locking position.

## Phase 3: Weapon Mechanics (Apr 18 ~19:30)

New per-weapon behaviors:

- **Shotgun** — 8 pellets in spread pattern, each does 1/3 base damage, independent headshot detection per pellet. Changed to semi-auto.
- **DMR** — Consecutive hit bonus: streak counter (max 5) reduces fire rate 0.5s -> 0.2s. Miss or 1.5s timeout resets. Changed to semi-auto.
- **Sniper charge-to-fire** — Hold to charge (0.8s), release to fire. Minimum 30% charge required. Damage scales 40-100%.
- **Headshots** — Hit point Y > enemy.y + 1.3 = headshot (3x damage). Golden "HEADSHOT" floating text on HUD.
- **Directional damage indicator** — Red wedge on HUD rotates to point at attacker, 1s fade.
- **Air control** — 30% ground acceleration while airborne.
- **Fire rate fix** — SMG 0.065s -> 0.10s, AR 0.12s -> 0.15s. Magazines were emptying in 2-3 seconds.
- **ADS mag dump bug** — `_update_weapon_bob` was resetting `can_shoot=true` every frame, bypassing fire rate cooldown. Fixed to check `shoot_timer`.

## Phase 4: VFX Polish (Apr 18 night — Apr 19 morning)

Visual effects for weapon feedback:

- **Muzzle flash** — Cross-shaped: 2 perpendicular bars with random rotation + white-hot core + 3-5 radial star rays. Parented to gun_pivot so flash tracks the gun during kick.
- **Bullet tracers** — Yellow glowing box projectile from barrel tip to hit point at ~150 m/s. Per-weapon linger time (SMG 0.15s, Sniper 1.5s).
- **Bullet fire trail** — 3-10 orange glow particles along bullet path, count scales with distance.
- **Bullet holes** — Dark cylinder decal + scorch ring at hit point, oriented to surface normal. 15s lifetime then shrink-and-free.
- **Impact debris** — 3-6 box fragments ejected along normal with spin and arc trajectory.
- **Sniper charge ring** — Red glowing square frames spawn every 0.25s while charging. Travel from muzzle to stock with exponential scaling (1x -> 8x). OmniLight3D glow. Multiple iterations to get the sizing right (ring must clear the scope body).

## Phase 5: Sight Polish (Apr 19 morning)

- **Marathon-style sights** — Big visible frame with tiny precise center dot. Red dot frame doubled in size, dot halved.
- **All weapons 1-4 now use red dot** — Shotgun and SMG moved from iron sights to red dot for consistency.
- **Enemy head hitbox** — Added actual head collision sphere (r=0.22 at Y=1.10) separate from body capsule. Previously headshots were Y-threshold only with no physical collision.
- **Sight z-fighting** — Added `sorting_offset` to all optic meshes (0.1-0.2) to render in front of gun body.
- **Charge ring z-order** — Ring edges get `sorting_offset=0.5` (higher than scope's 0.1).

## Architecture Decisions

| Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Split player_controller into 3 modules | 1920 lines was unmaintainable; gun mesh, VFX, and gameplay logic have no reason to be coupled |
| VFX object pooling | Muzzle flash/sparks/tracers were creating 5-10 meshes per shot with immediate GC pressure |
| Pending recoil buffer | Instant camera jerk felt broken; gradual application creates readable kick-then-return rhythm |
| Semi-auto for Shotgun/DMR | Full-auto let players dump entire mags with one click hold; intentional clicking rewards precision |
| Marathon-style sights (big frame, small dot) | Large frame aids fast target acquisition; small dot enables precise shots. Best of both worlds. |
| Head collision sphere | Y-threshold-only headshots had no physical basis; actual sphere enables proper raycast intersection |
